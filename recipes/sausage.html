Boiler Sausages

Description

A cooked sausage from the boiler is a popular food item because it's easy to prepare and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Boiling sausages is a quick and simple method that ensures they are cooked through evenly. Plus, boiling can help to remove excess fat, making it a healthier cooking option compared to frying.
Another great thing about boiled sausages is that they are versatile. They can be enjoyed on their own, sliced and added to salads or sandwiches, or used as a topping for pizzas and pasta dishes. The possibilities are endless! Boiled sausages are a delicious and convenient food that can be enjoyed at any time of day, making them a popular choice for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Ingredients

  • Sausages
  • Water
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Add Sausages to a hot pot with water.
  2. Remove the Sausages, once the water starts cooking.
  3. Optional: Season with ketchup.
