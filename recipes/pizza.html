Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is so delicious and good because it's made with fresh ingredients and baked to perfection. The dough is soft and chewy, with a crispy crust that's just the right amount of golden brown. The tomato sauce is rich and flavorful, complemented by the melted mozzarella cheese that's gooey and stringy. The toppings are fresh and vibrant, adding texture and depth to each bite. All of these elements come together to create a mouthwatering pizza that's sure to satisfy any craving.
One of the best things about making your own pizza is that you can customize it to your liking. You can choose your favorite toppings, experiment with different cheeses and sauces, and adjust the thickness of the crust to suit your preferences. Not only is it fun to make, but it's also a great way to enjoy a fresh and delicious meal that's perfect for sharing with friends and family. So why not try making this homemade pizza for yourself and experience the incredible taste and satisfaction that it brings?

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • 250g flour
  • 1 spoon instant yeast
  • 1 spoon salt
  • 150ml warm water
  • 3 spoons tomato sauce
  • 100g grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add the warm water to the bowl and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 220Â°C.
  6. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to your desired thickness.
  7. Place the rolled-out dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  8. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1cm border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over the tomato sauce.
  10. Add your desired toppings to the pizza.
  11. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
